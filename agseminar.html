SISSA Algebraic Geometry Seminar 2024–2025

This is the webpage of the SISSA Algebraic Geometry Seminar. The topics for the Spring Semester 2024–2025 will be Complex analytic methods, followed by Algebraic stacks. We meet on Mondays at 14:00 in Room 136.

Complex analytic methods – Detailed schedule

  • 20 January 2025, Žiga Gladek (SISSA): From smooth varieties to complex manifolds
  • 27 January 2025, Michele Graffeo (SISSA): Algebraic curves and 1-dimensional manifolds
  • 3 February 2025, Giordano Crimi (SISSA): Analytic coherent sheaves and GAGA
  • 10 February 2025, Lorenzo Cortelli (SISSA): Applications of GAGA theorems
We mainly follow the English version of GAGA and FAC.
